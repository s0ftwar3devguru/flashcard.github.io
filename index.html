Lion

Characterized as fearsome, courageous and majestic, the lion's strength and ferocity has earned it accolades such as 'King of Beasts' in many cultures.

  • Scientific Name : Panthera Leo
  • Average Weight : 265 to 550 Pounds
  • Average Lifespan : 10-15 years
  • Habitat : Sub-Saharan Africa

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Lions primarily eat large animals that weigh from 100 to 1,000 pounds (45 to 453 kilograms), such as zebra and wildebeest. Lions are the world's most social felines. They live in groups of related females, called prides, which may comprise several to as many as 40 individuals, including adults.
